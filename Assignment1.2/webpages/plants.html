Plants

You will be thrilled to find a wide veriaty of plants at our garden center. There must be one that suits your taste. Check the following categories for details


Vines
A vine is any plant with a growth habit of trailing or scandent stems, lianas or runners. The word vine can also refer to such stems or runners themselves, for instance, when used in wicker work.
Evergreens
A plant that retains green leaves throughout the year.
Flowering Shrubs
Any shrub that produces flowers.
Trees
In botany, a tree is a perennial plant with an elongated stem, or trunk, usually supporting branches and leaves. In some usages, the definition of a tree may be narrower, including only woody plants with secondary growth, plants that are usable as lumber or plants above a specified height.
Anuals
Recurring, done, or performed every year; yearly.
